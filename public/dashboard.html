Accessibility Statement
Compliance
WCAG 2.1 Level AAThis portal conforms to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1, Level AA as mandated by the Guidelines for Indian Government Websites (GIGW 3.0).
RPwD Act 2016 CompliantDesigned to be accessible to persons with visual, hearing, motor, and cognitive disabilities per the Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016.
Accessibility Features
Skip NavigationKeyboard users can jump directly to main content.
Text Resize (A− A A+)Three font size levels without loss of content or functionality.
5 Colour SchemesDefault, Light, High Contrast (black/yellow), Night Blue, Forest Green — catering to colour blindness and low vision.
10 Indian LanguagesFull UI translation across English, Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Punjabi, and Odia with location auto-detection.
Screen Reader CompatibleTested with NVDA, JAWS, and ORCA. All interactive elements have ARIA labels. Live regions announce dynamic updates.
Keyboard NavigationFull portal navigable using Tab, Enter, and arrow keys. Escape closes all modals and dropdowns.
Text SpacingIncreased line height (1.9×) and letter spacing available for users with dyslexia.
Big Cursor ModeEnlarged custom cursor for users with motor difficulties.
Feedback
For accessibility issues or suggestions, email accessibility@civiceye.in or call 1800-11-6666. We aim to respond within 2 working days.
